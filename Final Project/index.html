Final Project description


The idea for the final project is a very advanced version of the midterm project. With the skills we have learned since the midterm project we will be able to heavily advance the website and its complexity. Our objectives are to make a well structured online store that is 100% functional and would be able to be used at a commercial level.


Views


The first view will be the welcome screen, it will say something along the lines of "welcome to our online store" with a button to click to eanter the store.


The button that is pressed will take you straight to the browser view where you will be able to see every single product. There will be a way to sort the products as well as add them to the cart or view them individually.


When you click on a prodcut, that product will be put in focus and a description of the product will be brought up.


When you click the "check out" button you will be taken to the cart where you will be able to see all of your products and the prices


Next the user will be at the confirmation of payment screen where they will be required to put in their payment information to pay for their items. If the cart is empty then this screen will not be available for obvious reasons.


After the user has checked out and payed, they will be prompted to leave a review on any of the items that they purchased.
